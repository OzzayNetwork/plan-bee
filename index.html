About Us

The Plan Bee International Limited (TPB) is a systems developer and technology consulting business with extensive experience in the design, development and implementation of large scale technology projects. TPB prides itself with years of hands-on experience in some of the most critical systems implementations in Kenya and Africa.

Our innovative approach allows us to rapidly prototype client solutions and focus on providing the clients’ desire results throughout the course of the implementation. We adopt a consulting approach to create a collaborative implementation organization, led by the client.

Our Mission and Vision
